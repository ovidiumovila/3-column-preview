What is a SUV car?

A sport utility vehicle or SUV is a car classification that combines elements of road-going passenger cars with features from off-road vehicles,
such as raised ground clearance and four-wheel drive.

Where does the name "SUV" come from?

The first relevant usage of the term SUV was in advertising brochures for the full-sized 1974 Jeep Cherokee (SJ),
which used the wording "sport(s) utility vehicle" as a description for the vehicle.
