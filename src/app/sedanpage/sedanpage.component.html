What is a SEDAN car?

A SEDAN or saloon (British English) is a passenger car in a three-box configuration with separate compartments for engine, passenger, and cargo.

Where does the name "SEDAN" come from?

Sedan's first recorded use as a name for a car body was in 1912.
The name comes from a 17th-century development of a litter, the sedan chair, a one-person enclosed box with windows and carried by porters.
